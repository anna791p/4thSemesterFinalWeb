8 metric tons of trash enter the ocean each year. We are changing that.

Who are we?

GreenKayak is an environmental initiative, which aims to reduce the amount of waste in the water.

The main activity is to offer volunteers a free trip in our GreenKayaks, in return for collecting waste from the ocean.

During the last two years, several thousand volunteers used a GreenKayak in the harbours of Denmark, collecting more than 10 tons of waste.

Together, we can paddle for the oceans.
