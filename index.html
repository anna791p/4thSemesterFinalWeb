Who are we?

GreenKayak is a non-profit environmental initiative, which aims to reduce the amount of waste in the water.

The main activity is to offer volunteers a free trip in our kayaks, in return for collecting trash from the ocean. Our mission is to establish a global network of GreenKayaks.

The project is ongoing, and depends upon volunteers to help remove as much trash from the waterways as possible, to help preserve the beauty and life in the ocean.

During the last two years, several thousand volunteers used a kayak in the canals and harbours of Denmark, collecting more than 10 tons of waste.

Volunteer

As a volunteer, you become a part of the change - fighting for those without a voice!

Volunteering goes beyone just the kayak. With your voice we can spread awareness and encourage others to make more sustainable choices.

We are striving to keep the canals, harbours, seas and oceans of Denmark clean and free from waste, but we can’t do it without you.

With your help, we can remove tons of pollution from the water. In the last two years you've removed over 10 tons from the ocean! Can we beat it this year?
